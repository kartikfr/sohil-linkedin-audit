Quick Wins

Five Changes.
This Week.

01

Write the "About" Section

150–200 words. First person. Civil engineer to brand builder to solar VP. Include keywords: solar manufacturing, international sales, B2B, EPC, renewable energy.

Empty skills card → Compelling narrative that ranks in LinkedIn search
02

Pin 3 Media Features to the Featured Section

pv magazine India, EPR Magazine and "Torchbearer of Renewables" by Industrial Outlook. This social proof is sitting idle while every new visitor misses it.

Featured section empty → 3 credibility anchors visible in 2 seconds
03

Rewrite the Headline: Value Over Title

Your headline is the most-read line on your profile. Make it tell people what insight they gain by following you.

AVP @ GGBL | Driving Sales Growth → Scaling India's Solar Story | AVP Sales | International Markets | TOPCon | EPC
04

Stop Reposting. Start Experimenting.

As a sales leader, your credibility is built on how you think, not what you share. Shift from reposts to original content and try different formats: short video, written frameworks, POV takes on industry news. Video in particular is underused — a 60-second clip of you speaking candidly about solar will land harder than any company announcement. See what resonates, then double down on it.

Repost-heavy feed, no format strategy → Original content in your own voice, tested across formats
05

Fix the Hashtag Strategy

Currently using between 5 and 30 hashtags with no consistency. For B2B niche content, 5–8 focused tags outperform 30 generic ones every time.

Up to 30 random hashtags per post → #SolarIndia #RenewableEnergy #SolarEPC #B2BSales #MakeInIndia (5–8 max)
